Job Title: Data Analyst (Fresher)
Job Description:
We are seeking a detail-oriented and analytical Data Analyst to join our team. As a fresher, you will support data collection, cleaning, and visualization efforts to help drive business decisions. You’ll work closely with senior analysts and cross-functional teams to uncover insights from structured and unstructured data.
Key Responsibilities:
- Assist in data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL)
- Clean and validate datasets for accuracy and completeness
- Create basic dashboards and reports using Excel, SQL, or BI tools
- Support ad hoc analysis and data requests
- Collaborate with teams to understand data needs and deliver insights
Requirements:
- Bachelor’s degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related field
- Familiarity with Excel, SQL, and data visualization tools (e.g., Power BI, Tableau)
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Eagerness to learn and grow in a data-driven environment
